# Privacy Policy

**Game:** Mahjong: Legends of the Nile
**Developer:** Blurred Project
**Contact:** blurred.project.studio@gmail.com
**Effective date:** 17 May 2026
**Last updated:** 17 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data Mahjong: Legends of the Nile ("the Game", "we", "us") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By installing or playing the Game you agree to this Policy.

---

## 1. Summary (the short version)

- The Game is **free to play** and supported entirely by advertising. There are **no in-app purchases**, ever.
- We collect the **minimum data** needed to run the Game, show ads, sync your progress to the cloud (if you sign in), and keep our leaderboards working.
- We do **not** sell your personal data.
- We do **not** show personalised ads to users under 13.
- You can play **as a guest** without signing in or sharing personal information.

---

## 2. Information we collect

### 2.1 Data you provide directly

- **Display name** you type in (if you change the default guest name) — stored on PlayFab and shown on the global leaderboard.
- **Google account email + display name** — only if you choose to **Sign in with Google** to back up your progress. This lets us link your save to your Google account so you can restore it on another device. We do not receive your password.

### 2.2 Data collected automatically

- **Game progress and stats:** levels completed, scores, time taken, power-ups used. Stored on your device and (if signed in) synced to PlayFab cloud save.
- **Device information:** model, manufacturer, operating system version, language, country, screen size. Used to optimise the Game and triage bugs.
- **Crash reports and performance traces** via Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics (Google LLC). Includes stack traces, device state at crash time, and basic interaction events (level started, level completed, ad shown). No message content or personally identifying free-text fields are recorded.
- **Advertising identifier (AAID on Android, IDFA on iOS):** used by Google AdMob to serve ads. You can reset or limit this in your device settings (Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking).
- **IP address:** processed by ad networks and Firebase for fraud prevention and approximate geolocation (country only). Not stored by us.

### 2.3 Data we do NOT collect

- We do not collect contacts, photos, microphone, camera, calendar, SMS, call history, precise location, biometrics, or any health data.
- We do not require any device permission to function (no permissions are requested at install).

## 4. Advertising and personalised ads

Ads in the Game are served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your advertising identifier and approximate location (country) to show ads relevant to your interests.

**Your choices:**

- **EU, UK, Switzerland (GDPR):** on first launch you will see a Google-provided consent form. You can choose between personalised ads, non-personalised ads, or paying for an ad-free experience if offered. (We currently do not offer a paid ad-free option, so the choice is between personalised and non-personalised.) You can change your choice any time from the in-game **Settings → Privacy Options** link.
- **California (CCPA):** "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" is honoured. Use the same Settings → Privacy Options link to opt out.
- **All regions:** disable ad personalisation at the OS level — Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID". iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → toggle off "Allow Apps to Request to Track".

We never show personalised ads to users under 13. If our age signal indicates the user is under 13 (declared age in app, or country with default restrictions), only non-personalised ads are served.

## 5. Children and family policy

The Game is rated **PEGI 7 / ESRB Everyone 10+** and is appropriate for general audiences. It does not contain violence, gambling mechanics, or chat.

We comply with the **Google Play Families Policy** and **COPPA** (US). For users under 13:

- Account sign-in is disabled (guest play only).
- Only non-personalised ads are shown.
- No data is collected beyond what is strictly necessary to run the Game.

If you are a parent and believe we have inadvertently collected personal data from your child, email blurred.project.studio@gmail.com and we will delete the data within 30 days.

## 6. Data retention and deletion

| Data | Retention |
|---|---|
| Local save (on device) | Until you uninstall the Game |
| PlayFab cloud save | Until you request deletion or 24 months of inactivity, whichever is sooner |
| Firebase Analytics events | 14 months (Google default) |
| Firebase Crashlytics reports | 90 days |
| Leaderboard score entries | Permanent (top scores are part of the game's historical record); display name only |

### How to delete your data

Email **blurred.project.studio@gmail.com** with the subject line **"Data deletion request"** and your in-game display name or PlayFab player ID (Settings → About → Player ID). We will:

1. Delete your PlayFab cloud save and account record within 7 days.
2. Remove your leaderboard entries within 7 days.
3. Confirm deletion by email.

To delete local save data, uninstall the Game or use Settings → Reset Progress in-game.

You can also request a copy of your data ("data export request") using the same email.

## 7. Data security

- All network traffic (PlayFab, Firebase, AdMob) is encrypted in transit using HTTPS / TLS 1.2+.
- Cloud save data on PlayFab is encrypted at rest by Microsoft Azure.
- We do not store credit card or payment information (the Game has no in-app purchases).

No system is perfectly secure. If a data breach affecting your information occurs, we will notify affected users within 72 hours via in-game notification and email (if available).

## 9. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Access** — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
- **Rectification** — correct inaccurate data.
- **Erasure** ("right to be forgotten") — request deletion.
- **Restriction** — limit how we use your data.
- **Portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
- **Object** — object to processing for marketing or analytics purposes.
- **Withdraw consent** — for processing based on consent (e.g. personalised ads).

To exercise any of these rights, email **blurred.project.studio@gmail.com**. We respond within 30 days.

If you are in the EEA / UK and believe we are mishandling your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
